Clinic

Services we offer

We offer both in-patient and out-patient services.

Consultation

We accept both walk-ins and appointments.
We are staffed with highly expereinced and dedicated veterinarians to provide the best care to your pets.

Vaccinations

We offer a variety of vaccinations to boost your pet's immunity.
Every animal under our care is provided with a vaccination card to enable you to keep track of the vaccinations your furry friend is supposed to receive.
Vaccinations are an important part of both the pet's and its owners health so it is best to ensure they are kept up tp date with any vaccinations.

Emergency care

The emergency care unit is open 24 hours a day, everyday.
Therefore, in case of an emergency do not hesistate to bring in your pet to get the medical attention it needs.

Eye and dental care

We offer eye and dental services.

Grooming

Grooming is inclusive of: nail clipping, teeth brushing, bath and blowdry, dematting and deshedding, ear and eye cleaning, fur trimming and breed specified cuts.
